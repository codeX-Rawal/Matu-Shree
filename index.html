Our Story

A Legacy of Craftsmanship
Since 2009

Matu Shree Art & Craft was born in the heart of Jodhpur from a family's passion for transforming raw wood into living art. What began as a small workshop has grown into a celebrated brand — yet our hands still shape every piece with the same devotion as the first day.

We source only the finest teak, sheesham, and mango wood, letting the natural grain speak through each creation. Every joint is hand-fitted, every surface lovingly finished — because your home deserves nothing less than a masterpiece.
